Sample prompts

Prompt #4 — Handling "Your Rate Is Too High"
A client said my rate of $[amount] is too high. Help me write a professional response that:
- Doesn't apologize for my rate
- Reframes cost as ROI (reference the value they'd get)
- Offers ONE alternative (smaller scope, not lower rate)
- Keeps the door open without being desperate
Tone: confident, not defensive. Under 120 words.
Prompt #11 — LinkedIn Post (High-Engagement Format)
Write a LinkedIn post about [topic/result/lesson] using this structure:
Line 1: Bold, specific hook (no "I'm excited to share")
Lines 2-4: The story or data (3 short paragraphs)
Line 5: The counterintuitive takeaway
Line 6: Question to drive comments

Rules: No buzzwords, no "game-changer." Under 200 words.
Prompt #43 — "Unblock Me"
I'm stuck on [task]. Help me break through:
1. What's the smallest possible first step (under 15 minutes)?
2. What am I likely afraid of that I'm not admitting?
3. What's the cost of continuing to delay vs. doing it imperfectly now?
Then give me one specific instruction to start in the next 10 minutes.
